7 Endangered Languages and Where They Are

This is a website about endangered languages. It contains information about 7 endangered languages in the world at this moment.

This is the homepage tab, but there is also a map to show where they are, and other facts about them. Additionally, there is another tab for general information about endangered languages - all of these tabs are accessible through the navigation bar at the top of the screen.
Have fun reading!
